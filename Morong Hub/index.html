Bataan Nuclear Power Plant

The Bataan Nuclear Power Plant (BNPP) has become a popular tourist spot in the Philippines. Located in Morong, Bataan, it attracts visitors due to its historical significance and beautiful surroundings. Tourists can explore the plant's complex and learn about its design and equipment through guided tours. The BNPP's location in Bataan provides a picturesque backdrop with scenic landscapes and nearby beaches. The plant is also an educational destination, where students and researchers can study nuclear energy and its challenges. However, certain areas may have restricted access for safety reasons. If you plan to visit the BNPP, check with local authorities or official tourism channels for updated information on visiting hours and guidelines.

Bakas Higante

Bakas Higante, also known as the Giant Footprints, is a captivating tourist attraction located in Nagbalayong, Morong, Bataan, Philippines. Situated along the coastline, it offers visitors a unique rock formation resembling a giant footprint. The footprint of Bakas Higante measures 12x5 feet, while the well at the exact joint in the big toe of the footprint is 3x4 feet in length. With its picturesque views of the surrounding landscape and nearby sea, Bakas Higante has become a recognizable landmark and a popular destination for sightseeing and photography. While it primarily serves as a scenic spot, it provides an opportunity to appreciate nature's wonders, marvel at the intriguing geology, and immerse oneself in the serene coastal atmosphere of Nagbalayong, Morong, Bataan.

Anvaya cove

“Anvaya” means “family” in the ancient Sanskrit language, a return to meaningful family traditions is at the core of everything at Anvaya Cove. Anvaya Cove is a beautiful beach and nature club located in Morong, Bataan, Philippines. It offers a luxurious and tranquil retreat along the coastline of Subic Bay. With its pristine sandy beach, crystal-clear waters, lush landscapes, and a range of amenities including a golf course, swimming pools, sports courts, spa, and dining options, Anvaya Cove provides an exclusive and rejuvenating escape for its members and guests. The development also promotes environmental conservation and offers nature trails and bird-watching areas. It is a perfect destination for those seeking relaxation, natural beauty, and a high standard of living.

Alexa Waterpark

Nestled in the picturesque within Barangay Nagbalayong, Morong, Bataan, Alexa Waterpark stands as the crown jewel of the region's aquatic attractions. Boasting an expansive and immersive experience, it has earned a reputation as the largest and most thrilling water park in the area. Alexa Waterpark offers an impressive array of exhilarating inflatables and water obstacle activities that cater to visitors of all ages and preferences. From towering slides that plunge into crystal-clear pools to challenging obstacle courses that test one's agility and determination, the park provides a diverse range of attractions that guarantee non-stop excitement and fun. For adventure seekers, the park's massive wave pool serves as a pulsating hub of adrenaline-pumping action. Families with young children will find joy and delight in the safe and enjoyable kiddie pool area. Adding to the allure of Alexa Waterpark is the complimentary access to the stunning Juness Beach Resort, providing an added dimension of enjoyment and relaxation. Whether you're seeking an action-packed day of thrilling water adventures, quality time with loved ones, or a unique venue for team building activities, Alexa Waterpark promises an unforgettable experience. Embark on a grand aquatic adventure at Alexa Waterpark and create cherished memories that will last a lifetime.
